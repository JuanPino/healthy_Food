Legumes

Feeding yourself in a healthy way does not mean only what you eat but also how you eat. Legumes are healthy and very nutritious, but often add sausages and thus hundreds of calories. If you cannot give them a good technique is to pierce with a fork beforehand and then cooking them. Remove from the fat and then add them to burst pulses.
