Stir Fried Meal

The secret to good stir frying is not to put too much in the wok at the one time, if you do, the vegetables will sweat instead of frying. This recipe's for two people but if you're cooking for four, don't dump twice as much in, do it in two batches. Stir frying is a pair of Chinese cooking techniques for preparing food in a wok: chǎo and bào. The term stir-fry was introduced into the English language by Buwei Yang Chao, in her book How to Cook and Eat in Chinese.
